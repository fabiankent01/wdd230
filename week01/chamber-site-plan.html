Overview

Site Name: Benncima Chamber of Commerce

I choose this website because it is a representation of my community's chamber of commerce. I was immediately drawn to the website's clean, contemporary, and eye-catching design. I also saw that the website was lacking in material.

Site Purpose

By promoting public policies, encouraging trade, investment, and general business opportunities, providing business development services, and motivating members to uphold the highest standards of business ethics, the Benin City Chamber of Commerce seeks to advance and defend the interests of its members and the greater Edo State business community.

Scenarios

Below are some scenarios that provide questions to answers.

  1. I'm Interested in agro-allied investment in Edo State. What incentives does the Chamber offer? Where can I find details on opportunities?
  2. I'm a local farmer looking to expand in Edo State. How does the Chamber collaborate with farmers? Any ongoing programs for yield improvement and better prices?
  3. I'm considering opening a saloon in Edo state. Can the Chamber provide info on the industry's state, potential investment areas, and available support or partnerships?
